Located in the heart of Wasaga Beach. Inspired by the energy of the world’s longest freshwater beach, Wasaga Tattoo is a creative space built on craftsmanship, community, and self-expression. Artist Jake McQueen has designed the league's new logo, to be unveiled on March 28, 2026.
