We’d like to thank Monster Muffin for their sponsorship of the Renegade Derby Dames!! Monster Muffin is a Canadian-made athletic wear and uniform company that has been in business for over 21 years! With a passion for quality, design, and function, you are sure to be impressed by what they have to offer. Nearly everything is made in their small studio based in Springwater, Ontario (north of Toronto).  Although primarily focused on roller derby, their expertise knows no bounds.
